//REGION Wildshape reactions
// "DEN_DangerousMonsterReaction" causes non-Druid NPCs to say things like "oh look, another druid". It therefore doesn't make sense
// if played in reaction to e.g. a ranger summoning a wolf, because then they should know it's not a druid. If they see the wolf
// later it's fine, because they can still assume it's a shapeshifted druid at that point.
QRY
QRY_Crime_ShapeshiftDisturbance_IgnoreForNearbyCharacters((CHARACTER)_PartyMember, "DEN_DangerousMonsterReaction", (INTEGER)_CrimeID, (STRING)_Kind)
AND
DB_PlayerSummons(_PartyMember)
THEN
DB_NOOP(1);
//END_REGION
